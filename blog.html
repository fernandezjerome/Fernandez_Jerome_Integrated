17 April 2022

Gwen Marcel

This is the plant shop that I ALWAYS make sure I go to when I visit London. The place has a surprising amount of house plants, I got my string of hearts here. Parking can be tough so be careful—there is a small parking lot beside the shop and I find this shop easy to miss if you’ve never been. Once you get there, you’ll usually see a cute sidewalk chalkboard with a sweet planty message that is sure to delight you.
